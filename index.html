So how does this all work?

Peer-to-peer lending is exactly what the name suggests - peers, lending money to their peers. In return, the people borrowing the money repay the Lenders with interest. Our role in all of this is to run the peer-to-peer marketplace. This is where Borrowers list their personal loan applications, and Lenders choose which personal loans to fund.
